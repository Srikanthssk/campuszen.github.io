✦ Core Competencies

What You Will Master

Five skill domains that map directly to what security teams hire for. You won't just understand these topics — you'll build hands-on proof that you can execute them.

Network Security & Protocols

Master network architecture, firewalls, VPNs, IDS/IPS systems, and secure protocol design to protect organizational infrastructure end to end.

Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing

Learn offensive security — reconnaissance, scanning, exploitation, post-exploitation — and conduct full penetration tests using real-world methodologies.

Security Operations & Incident Response

Develop SOC skills — SIEM, threat hunting, log analysis, and incident response procedures for rapid containment and recovery after breaches.

Cryptography & Data Protection

Understand encryption algorithms, PKI, cryptographic protocols, and data-at-rest / data-in-transit protection strategies for sensitive systems.

Compliance & Risk Management

Apply security frameworks — ISO 27001, NIST, OWASP — and learn risk assessment methodologies used in enterprise GRC programs.
